About:

This is an enticing escape room board game designed to test your thinking skills alongisde your programming knowledge. This game has been developed to allow you to compete with a group of friends to battle to escape from a banks vault after breaking in. The challenge however is you need to break out... To do so you need to use different equipment to acquire a code to breakout.

Aim of the game and Contents:

Getting in was easy, can you break out? You have limited resources to be able to break out of the vault. With limited time you need to use the given resources to be able to retrieve the money and quietely break out of the vault befire anyone catches you. Using the resources you need to complete complex programming puzzles to release yourself from the vault.

  • 2 Small Boxes
  • 2 Small Padlocks
  • 2 USB's
  • Box
  • Paper
  • Hints and Tips

    • Rules and Tips:

    1. No external resources apart from those stated are allowed
    2. Brute force / tampering with resources to gain entry results in loss
    3. Be careful of what you touch. Evidence can be traced
    4. You will need a timing device and a computer for help
    5. Begin the timer as soon as you finish reading all instructions
    1. Elect a leader to overlook everything, you don't want to get stuck
    2. Collaboration will ultimately lead to success
    3. Everything in the box serves a purpose
    4. The best bank robbers can complete the heist in under 20 minutes
    5. Re-read instructions and hints, they'e there to help you
